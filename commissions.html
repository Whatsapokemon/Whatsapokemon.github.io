Whatsapokemon Commission Info

Sketch Commission

$15 (+$10 for additional character)

+$5 to add colour

Sketches are monochrome, and are usually less polished than typical linework commissions. Average maximum dimension is ~900 pixels on the longest side

Flat Colour Commission

$35 (+$20 for additional character)

Flat coloured commissions are clean lineart with flat colours on a simple background.

Shaded Commission

$50 (+$25 for additional character)

Shaded commissions are same as above, but with cel shading, highlights, and other prettifying effects.

Additional Info

  • Prices are all in US Dollars.
  • Feral and Anthro styles are both fine.
  • Additional character charge may be lowered if second character is significantly obscured or offscreen.
  • Backgrounds cost additional, depending on complexity.
  • Edits which require significant work will have additional charges.
  • Please make sure to have references available
  • Here is a list of fetishes that I won't draw. You can ask about any fetish which isn't on the list, but note that the list may be incomplete.
  • I reserve the right to refuse any commission for any reason I want.
